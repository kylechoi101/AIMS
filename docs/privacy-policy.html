Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

AIMS ("App Idea Makers Space") is a collaborative ideation platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use AIMS.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in via Apple or Google, we receive only the information you authorize (typically email and name).

User Content: Messages, ideas, and brainstorming content you post in Idea Rooms are stored in our database to enable real-time collaboration.

API Keys (BYOK): If you provide an AI API key, it is stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage (MMKV). We never transmit, store, or log your API keys on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the collaborative brainstorming service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To enable real-time messaging between room participants
  • To generate AI-powered responses using your own API key, sent directly from your device to the AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and chat messages are stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS), protected by PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Only authenticated room members can access room content.

API keys never leave your device. AI requests are made directly from your device to the respective AI provider's API endpoint.

4. Third-Party Services

AIMS integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Apple Sign-In / Google OAuth — authentication providers
  • OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI — AI inference (only when you provide your own API key; requests go directly from your device)

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

Your account and room data are retained as long as your account is active. You may leave rooms at any time to disassociate from their content. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

AIMS is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Export your brainstorming session data (via the in-app Share feature)
  • Revoke your API key access at any time by clearing it from Settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the app.
